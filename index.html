Our Products

PCC Panel

Power Control Centre Panels with current capacity up to 6300A. Widely used in industries like chemical, dairy, pharma, refrigeration, plastic, and more.

APFC Panel

Automatic Power Factor Control Panels improve efficiency by maintaining PF at ~0.99 lag with capacitor banks and programmable micro-controllers.

MCC Panel

Motor Control Centres with feeders, VFDs, PLCs, protection, and auto/manual modes for industrial motor management.

ACDB & DCDB Panel

Solar ACDB/DCDB panels for safe energy distribution, overload/short-circuit protection, and surge safety.

LDB Panel

Lighting Distribution Boards designed to safety standards for all types of industries and building projects.

ACVFD & DC Drive Panels

AC VFDs for conveyors, fans, pumps, etc. DC Drives provide high torque (up to 400%) for precision control applications.

Controller Based Panels

Manual control panels for temperature, flow, level, humidity with reliable automation of industrial processes.

PLC & Drive Panels

Fully automated PLC-based panels with HMI, Servo, and SCADA integration for industries like dairy, steel, pharma, rubber, and chemicals.
